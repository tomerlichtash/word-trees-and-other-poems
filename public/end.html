Word Trees and Other Poems is a free online publication for interactive visual poetry. It's a book, it's an experiment and it's a demo, for exploring Reading, Writing and Publishing of literary matter on the web, while using Google Charts' experimental Word Tree visualization tool as a writing environment constraint. No actual trees were harmed in the making these poems.
